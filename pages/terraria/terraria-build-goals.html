Overview

Crafting trees

Ranged upgrades

Your Megashark is well behind the rest of your kit now. Easiest gains are in the post-Plantera Dungeon; the best pre-Lunar guns come from events.

Summon upgrades

The Deadly Sphere Staff is fine, but a couple of post-Golem minions clearly outdo it.

One event, both upgrades

Martian Madness is a post-Golem event that drops the Xeno Staff (your summon upgrade) and the Xenopopper (a ranged upgrade) from the same Martian Saucer — so one event covers both. Your Piranha Gun is great for latching onto and killing the Saucers.

Endgame, when you're ready

Golem opens the Lunar events: cultists spawn at the Dungeon entrance, killing them summons the Lunatic Cultist, then the four Celestial Pillars, then Moon Lord. The Pillars drop fragments for best-in-slot gear — Vortex Beater / Phantasm for ranged and the Stardust Dragon for summons. No rush; the door's just open.

Potions & buffs worth farming

All buff potions use a Bottled Water base at a Placed Bottle or, better, an Alchemy Table (in the Dungeon — 33% chance to not consume ingredients). Flasks use the Imbuing Station from your Witch Doctor.
