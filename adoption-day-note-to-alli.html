Note to Alli, on Adoption Day

Written at 2:50 AM, the night of adoption day
October 25, 2025

My Sweet Love Lady,

As I watched you in the courtroom today, my heart felt like it might burst. Standing there beside you, seeing the joy on your face and the peace in your eyes, I couldn't help but think about how far we've come and how hard we've worked to build the life we have. We've fought for this stability, this love, and this sense of home. And now, here we are, married, with three beautiful children who all share our name, surrounded by love, and living in the home we built together.

You have been so strong, Alli. The kind of strong that doesn't just hold things together but makes everyone around you feel safe and cared for. You've grown so much and poured your heart into our family, and today, all of that hard work came full circle. I see it in the way our kids carry themselves, the way they trust us, the way they are learning emotional maturity from our example. The life we have today is a reflection of you; your faith, your love, and your relentless devotion.

· · ·

When I read my note to the kids this morning, I felt so connected to you. In that moment, everything I've ever dreamed of was right there in front of me. You, and the family we've built together. A few years ago, I was a lonely man who lived to work. Then I met you, and everything changed. You gave me purpose. You gave me a home. You gave me a family.

You are the heart of everything I hold dear. You are the center of our family; the reason it even exists. Every one of our children came from your love, and every bit of peace and wholeness I feel today traces back to you.

· · ·

When I told you earlier how amazing you are, you brushed it off and said, "I'm a mom, that's what we do." But you are not like everyone else. You are extraordinary. You show up every single day with love, grace, and strength that most people can only dream of. You make this family what it is. You make me who I am.

Tonight, as I write this, I feel secure, whole, and endlessly grateful. This day, this life, this love; it's everything I've ever wanted. Thank you for being my partner, my safe place, my Sweet Love Lady.

Forever and always,
Your Person
