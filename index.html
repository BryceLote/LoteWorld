about

The short version

Growing up in Texas I had two reference points for what work could mean. My mother spent forty years as a NICU nurse. My father built a career in IT and cybersecurity. Both of them stayed in their fields because they believed in what they were doing, not because it was easy or well-paid. That stayed with me even when I wasn't living up to it.

I got into serious trouble as a teenager. Hit a wall at eighteen that I didn't see coming and couldn't ignore. I spent several years in Hawaii putting myself back together, slowly and without a roadmap. The work of that period, the discipline it took, the willingness to start from nothing, shaped how I think about almost everything now.

I moved to Oklahoma in 2020. Built a career in operations and sales. I met the love of my life in 2023, and we got married in August of 2024. September 2025 our daughter Gianna was born, and that October I adopted Norah and Nehemiah. Our family officially became whole.

I work in healthcare technology now as an SDR at Calvient, a company building workflow automation for the organizations that serve patients regardless of ability to pay. I was employee twelve. Once again there is no playbook.

I organize my life around four things: family, faith, fitness, and finance. Everything else fits around those or it doesn't fit.
