About

The short version

I got into serious trouble as a teenager. Hit a wall at eighteen that I didn't see coming and couldn't ignore. I spent several years in Hawaii putting myself back together, slowly and without a roadmap. That period shaped how I think about almost everything now.

I moved to Oklahoma in 2020. Built a career in operations and sales. Married Alli in August 2024. Our daughter Gianna was born in September 2025, and that October I adopted Norah and Nehemiah.

These days I work in healthcare technology and I spend most of my free time outside, building something around the house, or scrolling through everything on TV until I'm too tired to actually watch anything.
