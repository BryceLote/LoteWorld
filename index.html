about

The short version

I grew up in Texas between two worlds; my mother was a NICU nurse for forty years, my father built a career in IT and cybersecurity. I got into serious trouble as a teenager, hit rock bottom at eighteen, and spent years in Hawaii putting myself back together piece by piece.

I moved to Oklahoma in 2020. Built a career in fitness and operations management. Married Alli in 2024. Our daughter Gianna was born in September 2025. In October I adopted Norah and Nehemiah and our family officially became whole. I work in healthcare technology now, at the intersection of the two fields I was raised in.

I organize my life around four things: family, faith, fitness, and finance. In that order.
